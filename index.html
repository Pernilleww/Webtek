Lorem Ipsum is CEO of Wallpapers and Ipsum. He runs a business of importing textiles, wallpapers and carpets. There a lot of competition in this industry, so mr Ipsum would like a professional website.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of this site is for stores to be able to find information about different products they can purchase form the suppliers through Wallpapers and Ipsum. They will also be able to find information about Wallpapers and Ipsum. The information will be shown with the use of photos and links that sends them to the different suppliers. The site will be organized with many different pages. There will be at least 5 pages, which will give the site a better structure and will be easy to navigate. The goal of the site is to make Wallpapers and Ipsum a better competitor in the industry, by making the business and its products more available and visible to the stores.

Audience

The main audience of this site are stores who want to purchase textiles, wallpapers and carpets which can be sold to individuals. Individuals may access the site, but they can't purchase or order anything since the site is created for stores ordering large quantities. They can however look through the site to see what might be available to purchase in stores.
