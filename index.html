Administrative Details

Lorem Ipsum is CEO of Wallpapers and Ipsum. He runs a business of importing textiles, wallpapers and carpets. There a lot of competition in this industry, so mr Ipsum would like a professional website.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of this site is for stores to be able to find information about different products they can purchase form the suppliers through Wallpapers and Ipsum. They will also be able to find information about Wallpapers and Ipsum. The information will be shown with the use of photos and links that sends them to the different suppliers. The site will be organized with many different pages. There will be at least 5 pages, which will give the site a better structure and will be easy to navigate. The goal of the site is to make Wallpapers and Ipsum a better competitor in the industry, by making the business and its products more available and visible to the stores.

Audience

The main audience of this site are stores who want to purchase textiles, wallpapers and carpets which can be sold to individuals. Individuals may access the site, but they can't purchase or order anything since the site is created for stores ordering large quantities. They can however look through the site to see what might be available to purchase in stores. Some of the challenges of creating this site might be the importance of showing all the products and information in a way so that the stores know what the business can supply and how they can order. It might also be challenging to show a lot of products from different pliers in a consistent way throughout the site. This is because different suppliers might have different design visions and varying commercial material available.

The content of the site and how it is organized

1. "About"

This page will have information about Wallpapers and Ipsum. How and when he started the business, and Wallpapers and Ipsum affiliates and suppliers.

2. "Contact us"

This page will contain information about location and contact information. There will be a google map where the location of the showroom and office will be located. There will also be a easy email form where the person can send orders or questions.

3. "Suppliers"

This page will contain information about all the suppliers of Wallpapers and Ipsum. Clicking on one of the suppliers will send you to another page where you can read more about the different products of that supplier. There will be some pictures for each supplier and a small about section.

4. "Products"

This page will show the different products Wallpapers and Ipsum can import form the different suppliers. Pillows, carpets, curtains, wallpapers and much more. When clicking on one of these products will send you to a different page which will show some collections of that product from the different suppliers.

4. "News"

On this page can Ipsum update his readers on the newest collections from his suppliers. The readers can be update on the latest trends etc.

Functional and non-Functional

Functional
  • The user should be able to get to any reference page
  • The user should be able to contact the business by filling out a email form
  • The location of the business should be shown by using google maps
  • The site should be user friendly
  • The products available should be easily found and documented
  • The information about different suppliers should be easily found
  • The reference pages should be available through a drop down menu visible at all times
  • The products will have their own page with categories for different products and a link to the page about the products supplier
  • The suppliers will have their own page connected to the page for the products
  • The site should be accessible on computers, tablets and phones
Non-Functional
  • Each reference page should be available within 2 clicks
  • The home page should be available within 1 click
  • The site should be scalable for accommodating different viewports
  • The site should be easily recognized and navigated by stores wanting to by their products
  • The site should be recognized by individuals as a site for stores, but still be easily navigated
  • contact information should be visible at the pages at all times
