Administrative Details

Lorem Ipsum is CEO of Wallpapers and Ipsum. He runs a business of importing textiles, wallpapers and carpets. There a lot of competition in this industry, so mr Ipsum would like a professional website.

Purpose and goals

The purpose of this site is for stores to be able to find information of different products they want to sell. They will also be able to find information about Wallpapers and Ipsum. They will get what they seek through photos and links that sends them to the different suppliers. The site will be organized with many different pages. There will be at least 5 pages, which will give the site a better structure and will be easy to navigate.

Audience

The main audience of this site are stores. Individuals may find the store, however they can't purchase or order anything. They must order through stores. Therefore, the site is mainly for stores.

The content of the site and how it is organized

1. "About"

This page will have information about Wallpapers and Ipsum. How and when he started the business, and Wallpapers and Ipsum affiliates and suppliers.

2. "Contact us"

This page will contain information about location and contact information. There will be a google map where the location of the showroom and office will be located. There will also be a easy email format where the person can send orders or questions.

3. "Suppliers"

This page will contain information about all the suppliers of Wallpapers and Ipsum. Clicking on one of the suppliers will send you to another page where you can read more about the different products of that supplier. There will be some pictures for each supplier and a small about section.

4. "Products"

This page will show the different products Wallpapers and Ipsum can offer. Pillows, carpets, curtains, wallpapers and much more. When clicking on one of these products will send you to a different page which will show some collections of that product from the different suppliers.

4. "News"

On this page can Ipsum update his readers on the newest collections from his suppliers. The readers can be update on the latest trends etc.

Functional and non-Functional

Functional
  • The user should be able to get to any reference page
  • Send a email from the contact us page
  • The location can be shown with a google map
  • There will be dropdown menus
Non-Functional
  • Fill out a form to send a email from the contact page
  • It should be easy to read. No uncomfortable colors
  • It should be able to do the required functions, such as load pages
  • It should be user friendly
